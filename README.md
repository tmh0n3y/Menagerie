# MENAGERIE

A short point-and-click visual novel about masked animals crafted by two roommates for Finnish Game Jam 2026.

## Download the executable and play:)

https://tmhoney.itch.io/menagerie

## The Story

A lone tent invites you in with a sense of familiarity lingering in the air. You are not sure why, but your legs are already moving you forward. Explore the tiny world of seven strangers and unmask your unique fate that may not be what you hope for.

## Controls

* \*\*Mouse\*\*: Hover over characters; \*\*Left Click\*\* to interact.
* \*\*Space and Arrows\*\*: Switch between dialogue pages and make choices.
* \*\*ESC\*\*: Open/Close Menu.
